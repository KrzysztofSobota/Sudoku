Sudoku game

This game come from Japan and is a mathematical brain teaser.

Big square is made from 81 small square fields (9 rows, 9 columns) and it has got two main 9-elements diagonals. Furthermore red lines show additional division by 3x3 squares.

The task is filling all the space with digits 1-9 in a such way that in every:

  • row,
  • column,
  • main diagonals,
  • 3x3 square,

none of these digits will be repeated.

Example of properly filled board game is in the centre of the screen. To play a game please choose level from the list and click PLAY button (both in "Options menu").

Options menu
You want to play for:
fun only (time off)
record (time on)

Time is counting from the moment of click PLAY button.

Number of empty cells (squares) on the board depends on chosen level of the game.
