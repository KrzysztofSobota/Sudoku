Sudoku game

This game come from Japan and is a mathematical brain teaser. Big square is made from 9 rows, 9 columns and it has got two 9-elements diagonals. Furthermore red lines show additional division by 3x3 squares. The task is fill all the space with digits 1-9 in a such way that in every:
  • row,
  • column,
  • main diagonals,
  • 3x3 square,
none of these digits will be repeated. Example of properly filled board game is in the centre of the screen. To play a game please choose level from the list and click PLAY button (both in "Options menu").
